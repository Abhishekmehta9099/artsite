PRODUCTS ON WHICH WE ARE DEALING

TT BAR

The Ten Tola Gold Bar (TT Bar) is a traditional gold bar commonly used in South Asia and the Middle East. It weighs 116.64 grams.

PAMP BAR

PAMP Gold Bars are premium-quality gold bullion bars produced by PAMP Suisse, a renowned precious metals refiner based in Switzerland. These bars are recognized globally for their exceptional purity, craftsmanship, and security features.

995 KILO BAR

A 995 Gold Kilo Bar is a large-format gold bullion bar typically used by institutional investors or high-net-worth individuals for significant investments in gold. The "995" refers to the bar's purity, meaning it contains 99.5% pure gold, with the remainder being alloy metals for added strength.

999.9 KILO BAR

A 999.9 Gold Kilo Bar is a high-purity gold bullion bar ideal for investors and institutions seeking to store substantial wealth in gold. The "999.9" indicates 99.99% pure gold, often referred to as 24-karat gold or "four nines" fine gold.
